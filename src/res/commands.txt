List of Commands:

1. help - Displays the list of available commands.
2. exit - Exits the program.
3. logout - Logs out the currently logged-in user.
4. transfer - Transfers money to another user.
5. withdraw - Withdraws money from the account.
6. deposit - Deposits money into the account.
7. interest - Applies interest to accounts (only for admin accounts).
8. database - Displays database information (only for admin accounts).
9. info - Displays information about the currently logged-in account.
10. delete - Deletes the logged-in account (confirmation required).
